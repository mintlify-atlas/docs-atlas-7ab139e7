## Account Creation

CompuTécnicos supports multiple registration methods to get you started quickly.

### Registration Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email Registration" icon="envelope">
    Traditional account creation with email and password
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google OAuth" icon="google">
    Quick sign-up using your Google account
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Creating an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    Click **Registrarse** in the top navigation or visit `/registro.php`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Registration Method">
    - **Email**: Fill out the registration form with your details
    - **Google**: Click the Google sign-in button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Provide required information:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Password (minimum 6 characters)
    - Phone number (optional)
    - Delivery address (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify and Login">
    After registration, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the homepage
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Google OAuth Registration

When you register with Google:
- Your name and email are automatically populated
- A secure account is created without needing a password
- Your Google profile photo is used as your avatar
- You can still add phone and address details later

<Note>
Google OAuth accounts are linked to your Google email. You can manage the connection through your Google account settings.
</Note>

## Profile Management

Access your profile by clicking your name in the navigation or visiting `/perfil.php`.

### Profile Sections

Your profile page has three main tabs:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Personal Information">
    Update your basic account details:
    - **Name**: Your full name
    - **Email**: Contact email (used for order notifications)
    - **Phone**: Contact number for delivery coordination
    - **Address**: Default shipping address
    - **Profile Photo**: Upload a custom avatar (JPG, PNG)
    
    <Note>
    Email changes may require verification. Your email is used for password resets and order updates.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Security">
    Manage your account security:
    - **Change Password**: Update your login password
    - **Active Sessions**: View and manage logged-in devices
    - **Two-Factor**: Currently not available
    
    **Password Requirements**:
    - Minimum 6 characters
    - Must enter current password to change
    - New password must be confirmed
    
    <Warning>
    Changing your password will log you out of all other devices for security.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Order History">
    View all your previous orders:
    - Order number and date
    - Order status and tracking
    - Total amount paid
    - Download invoices (PDF)
    - Reorder with one click
    
    See [Order Tracking](/features/order-management) for more details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Updating Personal Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Click your name in the top navigation bar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select 'Información Personal' Tab">
    This opens the personal information editor
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Fields">
    Update any field you want to change:
    ```html
    Name: John Doe
    Email: john@example.com
    Phone: +57 300 123 4567
    Address: Calle 123 #45-67, Bogotá
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Guardar Cambios** to update your profile. Changes are saved immediately via AJAX.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Uploading Profile Photo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Cambiar Foto'">
    In the personal information tab, click the camera icon or "Cambiar Foto" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Image">
    Choose a JPG or PNG file from your device. Maximum file size: 5MB
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Crop and Adjust">
    Use the image preview to ensure your photo looks good
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload">
    Click **Subir** to save your new profile photo. Old photos are automatically removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Security Tab">
    In your profile, click the **Seguridad** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    For security, you must verify your current password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Provide your new password (minimum 6 characters) and confirm it
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Actualizar Contraseña**. You'll be logged out of other devices automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Password Reset (Forgot Password)

If you forget your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Olvidé mi contraseña'">
    On the login page, click the forgot password link
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide the email address associated with your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    You'll receive a password reset link valid for 1 hour
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    Click the link in the email and enter your new password
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Password reset tokens expire after 1 hour for security. The system limits reset requests to 3 per 15 minutes to prevent abuse.
</Warning>

## Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time.

<Warning>
**This action is irreversible!** Deleting your account will:
- Remove all your personal information
- Cancel any pending orders
- Delete your order history
- Remove your reviews and comments
- Cannot be undone
</Warning>

### How to Delete Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Account Deletion Page">
    Navigate to `/eliminar_cuenta.php` or find the link in your profile settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Your Identity">
    Enter your current password to verify
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Acknowledge Warning">
    Read and confirm you understand data will be permanently deleted
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete Account">
    Click **Eliminar mi cuenta permanentemente**. You'll be logged out immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Session Management

### Remember Me

When logging in, you can check **Recordarme** to stay logged in for up to 30 days.

**How it works**:
- A secure token is stored in your browser
- You stay logged in even after closing the browser
- Token is automatically renewed with each visit
- Changing your password invalidates all remember tokens

### Logging Out

Click **Cerrar Sesión** in the navigation to log out:
- Ends your current session
- If "Remember Me" was enabled, it's also cleared
- You'll be redirected to the homepage

<Tip>
For security on shared computers, always log out manually instead of relying on browser close.
</Tip>

## Privacy & Data

### What We Store

Your account includes:
- **Required**: Name, email, password (encrypted)
- **Optional**: Phone, address, profile photo
- **Automatic**: Registration date, order history, reviews

### Data Access

You can view and export:
- Your personal information (profile page)
- Order history and invoices
- Product reviews you've written

- Your right to access, modify, and delete personal data
- Secure password storage (bcrypt hashing)
- Optional data collection (phone/address)
