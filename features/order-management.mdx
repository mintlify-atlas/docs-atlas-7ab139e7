## Overview

The CompuTécnicos order management system provides complete visibility into your purchase history. Track orders in real-time, view detailed status updates with timestamps, and download invoices for completed purchases.

<Note>
  You must be logged in to view your orders. The system only displays orders associated with your account.
</Note>

## Accessing Your Orders

Navigate to your orders page:

1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your profile menu
3. Select **"Mis Pedidos"** (My Orders)
4. View all orders sorted by most recent first

## Order Display

### Order Card Layout

Each order is displayed in a detailed card showing:

**Header Section:**
- **Order ID** (e.g., Pedido #123)
- **Order date and time**
- **Current status badge** with color coding
- **Total amount** in COP

**Body Section:**
- **Product list** with images and quantities
- **Invoice download** button (for paid orders)
- **Order tracking** progress bar
- **Status history** timeline
- **Tracking number** (when shipped)

### Status Badge Colors

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Pendiente | Orange | Action required (payment) |
| Pagado | Blue | Payment complete |
| En Preparación | Purple | Being processed |
| En Camino | Teal | Out for delivery |
| Entregado | Green | Completed successfully |
| Cancelado | Red | Order cancelled |

## Order Tracking

### Visual Progress Bar

The tracking system includes a visual progress indicator:

<Accordion title="Progress Bar Features">
  **Progress Percentage:**
  - Calculated based on current order stage
  - Displayed as a percentage (0-100%)
  - Visual bar fills from left to right

  **Stage Indicators:**
  - 5 circular step markers
  - Completed steps: filled with checkmark
  - Current step: highlighted and animated
  - Future steps: grayed out

  **Timestamps:**
  - Each completed step shows date and time
  - Future steps show "—" placeholder
</Accordion>

### Tracking Stages

Orders progress through these stages:

1. **Pedido recibido** (Order Received)
   - Initial order creation
   - Timestamp: when order was placed

2. **Pagado** (Paid)
   - Payment confirmation
   - Timestamp: when PayPal payment completed

3. **En preparación** (In Preparation)
   - Order being packed
   - Timestamp: when admin marks as preparing

4. **En camino** (In Transit)
   - Shipped to customer
   - Timestamp: when marked as shipped

5. **Entregado** (Delivered)
   - Successfully delivered
   - Timestamp: when marked as delivered

<Tip>
  Click on the tracking progress bar or "Ver historial" button to expand detailed status history.
</Tip>

## Delivery Estimates

The system provides estimated delivery dates:

### Calculation Method

<Accordion title="Delivery Time Estimation">
  **Based on Current Status:**
  - **Pendiente**: +3 days from now
  - **Pagado**: +2 days from now
  - **Preparación**: +36 hours from now
  - **Enviado**: +1 day from now
  - **Entregado**: Shows "✓ Entregado"

  **Geographic Considerations:**
  - Bogotá: 1-2 days
  - Other cities: 3-5 days
  - Displayed as: "Est. entrega DD/MM HH:mm"
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Delivery estimates are calculated from the current time and are subject to carrier performance and location.
</Note>

## Status History

View complete order timeline:

### History Display

1. Click **"Ver historial"** button on any order
2. Detailed timeline expands below tracking bar
3. Each event shows:
   - **Status name** (e.g., "Pagado")
   - **Date and time** of status change
   - **Optional comment** from admin (if any)

### History Entry Format

```
Pagado — 15/03/2024 14:30 (Pago confirmado vía PayPal)
En preparación — 16/03/2024 09:15 (Pedido en proceso)
```

<Tip>
  Admin comments in the history provide additional context about order processing, such as special handling notes or delay explanations.
</Tip>

## Invoice Management

### Downloading Invoices

For paid and completed orders:

1. Locate the **"Descargar factura"** button
2. Button appears for orders in states:
   - Pagado
   - Preparación
   - Enviado
   - Entregado
3. Click to download PDF invoice
4. Opens in new tab or downloads directly

<Note>
  Invoices are only available after payment confirmation. Pending orders cannot generate invoices.
</Note>

### Invoice Features

Generated invoices include:
- Company information and logo
- Customer details
- Order ID and date
- Itemized product list
- Subtotals, taxes, and total
- Payment method
- Electronic signature (for Colombian compliance)

<Tip>
  Keep your invoices for warranty claims and accounting purposes. You can download them multiple times.
</Tip>

## Tracking Number

When orders are shipped:

### Tracking Box Display

A special tracking section appears showing:
- **"Número de guía"** label
- **Tracking code** in monospace font
- **Copy button** for easy copying

### Using Tracking Numbers

1. Wait for order status to change to "Enviado"
2. Tracking number appears in order details
3. Click **"Copiar"** button to copy to clipboard
4. Use on carrier's website to track shipment
5. Button shows **"✓ Copiado"** confirmation

  - **Service level selected**

  Common carriers:
  - Servientrega
  - Coordinadora
  - Envía
  - Interrapidísimo

  Contact support if you need carrier information.
</Accordion>

## Order States in Detail

### Pendiente (Pending)

**What it means:**
- Order created but not yet paid
- Awaiting PayPal payment confirmation

**Customer action:**
- Complete payment via PayPal
- Payment page available in checkout

**Duration:**
- Order automatically cancelled if not paid within 24 hours

### Pagado (Paid)

**What it means:**
- Payment successfully processed
- Order confirmed and ready for preparation

**What happens next:**
- Inventory updated
- Order queued for fulfillment
- Invoice generated

**Duration:**
- Typically moves to "Preparación" within 4-8 hours

### En Preparación (In Preparation)

**What it means:**
- Items being picked and packed
- Quality checks performed
- Shipping label created

**Duration:**
- Usually 4-12 hours
- May be longer for large orders

### En Camino (Shipped)

**What it means:**
- Package handed to carrier
- Tracking number active
- In transit to delivery address

**What happens:**
- Receive tracking number
- Can track on carrier website
- Expected delivery within 1-5 days

### Entregado (Delivered)

**What it means:**
- Package successfully delivered
- Signed for by recipient
- Order complete

**After delivery:**
- Can still download invoice
- Warranty period begins
- Can leave product review

### Cancelado (Cancelled)

**What it means:**
- Order cancelled before fulfillment
- No charge if cancelled before payment
- Refund issued if already paid

**Reasons:**
- Customer request
- Payment failure
- Stock unavailable
- Admin cancellation
