### What Affects Purchase Price

<Accordion title="Condition" icon="star">
  - **Excellent**: Like new, no cosmetic damage → Best price
  - **Good**: Minor wear, fully functional → 80-90% of excellent price
  - **Fair**: Visible wear, some issues → 50-70% of excellent price
  - **Poor/Damaged**: Non-functional or major damage → Parts value only
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Age" icon="clock">
  - **< 1 year**: Current generation → Premium pricing
  - **1-3 years**: Recent models → Standard pricing
  - **3-5 years**: Older but usable → Reduced pricing
  - **> 5 years**: Legacy hardware → Limited value
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Completeness" icon="box">
  - **Complete**: Original box, accessories, documentation → +10-15%
  - **Device Only**: No accessories → Standard price
  - **Incomplete**: Missing components → Reduced price
</Accordion>

<Tip>
**Store Credit Bonus**: Accept payment as store credit and receive a 10% bonus. Great if you're planning to upgrade!
</Tip>

### What's Covered

✅ Workmanship defects  
✅ Properly installed components failing due to installation  
✅ Same issue recurring within warranty period  
✅ Re-service at no additional cost  

### What's Not Covered

❌ Physical damage after service  
❌ Liquid damage  
❌ Modifications by other parties  
❌ Normal wear and tear  
❌ Software issues (unless part of original service)
