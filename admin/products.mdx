Manage your product catalog, inventory, pricing, and special promotions through the Products administration panel.

## Accessing Products

Navigate to **Admin Panel** > **Products** to view your complete product catalog.

The products list shows:
- Product image
- Product name
- Category and brand
- Current price
- Stock level (color-coded: red for out of stock, yellow for low stock, green for adequate stock)
- Status badges (Featured, New, On Sale)

## Adding a New Product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the New Product form">
    Click the **+ New Product** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in basic information">
    - **Name**: Product name (required)
    - **Description**: Detailed product description
    - **Price (COP)**: Product price in Colombian pesos (required)
    - **Stock**: Available quantity (required)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select category and brand">
    - **Category**: Choose from the dropdown (required)
    - **Brand**: Choose from the dropdown (required)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload images">
    - Click **Choose Files** and select one or more product images
    - Multiple images will create a product gallery
    - At least one image is required
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set special status (optional)">
    Check **¿Producto en oferta?** to mark the product as on sale.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the product">
    Click **Save Product** to add the product to your catalog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Products marked as "On Sale" can have an expiration date set. After the expiration date, the offer badge will change to "Oferta vencida" (Expired offer).
</Note>

## Editing Existing Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the product">
    Use the search bar (🔍 icon) to find products by name, or scroll through the list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the edit form">
    Click the **Edit** button next to the product you want to modify.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update product information">
    All fields from the creation form are available:
    - Name, description, price, stock
    - Category and brand
    - Offer status
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add or replace images">
    - Select new images to add to the gallery
    - Existing images are displayed below the upload field
    - New images will be added to the existing gallery
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save Changes** to update the product.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The edit modal shows a preview of the current product images and the complete gallery. You can review all existing images before uploading new ones.
</Tip>

## Product Status Badges

Products can display multiple status badges:

- **⭐ Destacado** (Featured): Highlighted products that appear prominently
- **NUEVO** (New): Products marked with a "new until" date that hasn't expired
- **OFERTA** (On Sale): Products currently on sale
- **Oferta vencida** (Expired offer): Products with expired sale dates

## Stock Management

Stock levels are color-coded in the product list:

- 🔴 **Red badge**: 0 units (out of stock)
- 🟡 **Yellow badge**: 1-5 units (low stock warning)
- 🟢 **Green badge**: 6+ units (adequate stock)

<Note>
  Stock is automatically adjusted when orders are processed. See [Inventory Management](/admin/inventory) for detailed stock tracking.
</Note>

## Deleting Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Delete">
    Click the **Delete** button next to the product you want to remove.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    A confirmation dialog will appear showing the product name.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete deletion">
    Confirm to permanently delete the product and all associated images.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a product is permanent and will:
  - Remove the product from the catalog
  - Delete all product images from the server
  - Remove all gallery images associated with the product
  - This action cannot be undone
</Warning>

## Searching Products

Use the search bar at the top of the products list to filter by product name in real-time. The search works instantly as you type.

## Tips for Product Management

<Tip>
  **Image best practices:**
  - Use high-quality images with a white or neutral background
  - Multiple angles help customers make informed decisions
  - Keep images under 2MB for faster loading
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Pricing strategy:**
  - Update prices regularly based on supplier costs
  - Use the offer feature for temporary promotions
  - Set realistic stock levels to avoid overselling
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Categorization:**
  - Ensure products are in the correct category for easy customer navigation
  - Keep brand information consistent for filtering and search
</Tip>
